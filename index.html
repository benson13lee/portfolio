01 / About

I help projects and moments land.

I've spent ten years producing, coordinating, and communicating across the entertainment world. I started in a public relations agency at Ogilvy, ran digital and media work for the Taiwan Equality Campaign during one of the most pivotal moments in Taiwanese civil rights history, and spent five years at Sony Pictures Entertainment — first as Publicity Supervisor, then as Marketing Supervisor for Partnerships and Promotions.

My work has always sat in between things: between creative and logistics, between brand and audience, between the thing being made and the people it's being made for. At Sony, that meant coordinating gaming co-promotions with PlayStation, Capcom, Ubisoft, PUBG, and Garena — localizing global deals and finding new ways to extend them in the Taiwan market. I like that middle place. I am the bridge, and I build the rapport. It's where most of the actual work happens.

I recently moved to Vancouver, and I'm building what comes next — open to full-time roles, freelance projects, and the occasional microphone.
